Center for Global Education helps clients plan with confidence in COVID-19 era.

Effective September 6, 2020 all clients will be screened for respiratory symptoms and have their body temperature taken as a precautionary measure to reduce the spread of COVID-19.

Every client will be screened, including having his or her temperature taken, when visiting office with an appointment. They will be screened using a touchless forehead/ temporal artery thermometer. The temperature will be documented, and the record will be maintained.

Anyone who has a fever at or above 100.4 degrees Fahrenheit or who is experiencing symptoms of the virus will be sent back and consultation will be done via telephone.
